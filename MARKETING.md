# ToolHopp Viral Launch Strategy

## 1. Social Media Posts

### Platform: TikTok / Instagram Reels (The Hook)
*   **Visual:** A person struggling with a rusted, expensive tool they bought once vs. a "Hopper" arriving with a brand-new tool and a smile.
*   **Caption:** "Stop buying tools you only use once. Rent from your neighbor and save 80%. Or become a Hopper and get paid to deliver! 🛠️💨 #ToolHopp #SideHustle #DIY #ConstructionLife"

### Platform: Twitter/X (The Vision)
*   **Content:** "The gig economy was for rides and food. Now, it's for the foundation of our world: Tools. We're launching ToolHopp to empower local communities and job sites. Rent anything. Deliver everything. 🏗️🏠 #BuildTheFuture #ToolHopp"

### Platform: Facebook Groups (Local Target)
*   **Content:** "Hey [City Name]! Starting a DIY project this weekend? Don't go to the big box store. Check out ToolHopp. We've got local rentals for half the price, delivered to your door. Support your neighbors! [Link]"

## 2. Google Play Store (ASO)
*   **Title:** ToolHopp: Tool Rental & Delivery
*   **Short Description:** Rent tools from neighbors. Earn money as a Hopper delivery driver.
*   **Keywords:** tool rental, construction tools, side hustle, delivery app, home improvement, DIY rentals, local marketplace.

## 3. Launch Timeline
*   **Week 1:** "Coming Soon" teaser posts on IG/TikTok.
*   **Week 2:** Open "Hopper" registration (Get your fleet ready!).
*   **Week 3:** Official App Store & Web launch.
*   **Week 4:** Partnership announcement (Potential U-Haul/Uber pilot).
